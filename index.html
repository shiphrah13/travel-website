About Us

The captivating journey of Kashmir started decades ago when the founder of our company first laid eyes on the splendour of the land. We took it upon ourselves to make sure the mesmerising landscape, the welcoming Kashmiri people, as well as the pristine lakes and snow capped mountains, are experienced by the entire world.

Our organisation is the first and best travel company in Kashmir. We make sure to provide our clients with real life experiences that are not usually offered. We engage local specialists who ensure that every trip is customized, responsibly planned, and aligned to the local customs and surroundings, in the least intrusive way possible.

People do not consider a trip to Kashmir as a leisure holiday. It is rather, an insightful journey, and it holds the capacity to alter the inner self of an individual. We are sure that you will fall in love with Kashmir and will appreciate the pristine Dal Lake, Mughal gardens, calm mountain estates, splendid local shops, and other fascinating holiday destinations.
